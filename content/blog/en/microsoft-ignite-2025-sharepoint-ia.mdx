Microsoft Ignite 2025 made it clear: **SharePoint is no longer "just" an intranet or document repository — it is becoming an AI-driven strategic hub.**

This paradigm shift has concrete implications for all intranet projects currently underway or planned.

---

## AI Becomes the Engine of Your SharePoint Sites

Copilot reads, summarizes, connects, and prioritizes your SharePoint content. It transforms static information into **actionable information**.

But be careful: a poorly structured content architecture prevents AI from working properly. AI amplifies what already exists — the good and the bad.

---

## Governance Is Back to Being a Priority

This is the paradox of this evolution: the more powerful AI becomes, the more critical the quality of the document base becomes.

Disorganized information architecture, duplicate content, missing metadata **will all be amplified by AI**.

> "AI doesn't work miracles if the foundation isn't solid."

Microsoft Ignite 2025 offers an opportunity: use this momentum to finally restructure what should have been restructured long ago.

---

## Your Intranet Becomes an Intelligent Workspace

The announced evolutions transform the intranet from passive consultation to **active workspace**:

- **Page content generation** via Copilot
- **Knowledge Agents** capable of classifying and surfacing information
- We no longer "browse" the intranet, we work from the intranet.

---

## What Does This Change Concretely?

Your intranet becomes a strategic tool for centralizing and leveraging information — far beyond simple file storage.

For digital transformation teams, this is the perfect moment to:

- ✅ Reorganize existing content
- ✅ Establish clear content governance
- ✅ Co-build concrete AI use cases with business teams

---

## My Conviction

SharePoint opens the way to smarter collaboration… but **only if you're ready to structure your content upfront**.

Organizations that invest now in document governance and information architecture will be the ones who get the most out of AI!

The others risk finding themselves with a powerful tool... on a fragile foundation.
