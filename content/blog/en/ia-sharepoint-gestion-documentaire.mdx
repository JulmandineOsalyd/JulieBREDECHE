In 2025, Microsoft is embedding AI at the core of its environment, with a clear ambition: to make SharePoint far more than a simple storage space.

The goal is to turn it into a true intelligent assistant capable of unlocking the value of all the information sitting inside your documents.

Rather than a single product, this is a set of capabilities woven into the Microsoft 365 ecosystem. Their mission: make sense of your files and automate repetitive tasks.

---

## 1. SharePoint Premium (formerly Microsoft Syntex)

An advanced document management platform that leverages AI to:

- **Automatically extract key data** (dates, amounts, clauses)
- **Assemble templates** or trigger approval flows
- **Apply your security and compliance policies**

![SharePoint Syntex becomes SharePoint Premium](/images/blog/SharePoint-Premium.png)

---

## 2. Copilot for Microsoft 365

Embedded directly into the apps you use every day (Word, Outlook, Teams, SharePoint…), Copilot becomes a cross-functional work assistant.

In SharePoint, it helps you:

- **Unify your content** by using SharePoint as the documentary backbone to fuel other M365 tools
- **Generate and synthesize** relevant content from the files and pages of your SharePoint sites

![M365 Copilot interface](/images/blog/Copilot-M365.png)

---

## 3. SharePoint Knowledge Agents

> 💡 Available in Public Preview since September 2025.

Small specialized assistants you can deploy on a specific SharePoint site, page, list, or library.

They allow you, for example, to:

- **Search information** from a targeted documentary base (HR, legal, support…)
- **Keep your Intranet content up to date** (broken links, outdated pages, automated FAQs)
- **Apply metadata automatically** to prepare your documents to be exploited by Copilot

![Knowledge Agents: automatic organization of a SharePoint library](/images/blog/Knowledge-Agents.png)

---

## 4. ⚠️ A Few Limits to Keep in Mind

While promising, AI in SharePoint is not magic and there are a few watch points to keep in mind:

- **An ecosystem limited** to the Microsoft environment
- **An additional cost**: Copilot is offered as an add-on (€30 / user / month), and some advanced SharePoint Premium features are billed pay-as-you-go
- **A dependency on the quality of your content**: a messy documentary base leads to disappointing results

---

## Conclusion

With SharePoint, Microsoft is turning document management into a true strategic lever: automation, stronger governance, and intelligent exploitation of knowledge.

By combining Copilot and Knowledge Agents, **your content is no longer just stored, it becomes a living resource** that fuels your decisions, your projects, and your teams.
